About Eirene

Empowering Mental Wellness Through AI

Bridging the gap between technology and mental health care with culturally-aware, accessible solutions for Indian youth.

Mental health remains a significant challenge for Indian youth, with stigma, accessibility, and cultural barriers preventing many from seeking help. Eirene leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide personalized, culturally-sensitive mental wellness support.

Our platform combines creative therapy, proactive mood monitoring, and multilingual support to create a safe space where young Indians can explore their mental health without judgment or fear.
